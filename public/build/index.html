Questions

Good questions.

Is $100 all I pay? +

No. The $100 is a deposit that covers the AI build costs to get started. The full price is whatever we agree on the call, and you pay the rest at the end when the app is delivered.

What if I don't like it? +

We agree what "done" looks like before I build, so you know exactly what you're getting. I keep working until it matches what we scoped together.

Can it really install on my phone? +

Yes. These install to your home screen and open like a normal app (it's a PWA), and they also work in any browser. No app store, no download approval needed.

Do I own it? +

Yes. The app is yours, code and all. No lock-in.

How long does it take? +

Most builds are days, not months. We'll set a realistic timeline on the call based on what you want.

What can't you build? +

Anything that needs deep native phone features (heavy camera, Bluetooth) or a huge complex backend. If your idea isn't a fit, I'll tell you straight on the call before you pay anything.
